Ozcorp Scientific Unveils GENOS to Propel Biotechnology Research Through Tailored Bioinformatics Services

April 18, 2024
SAN JUAN, Puerto Rico – April 14, 2024 – Ozcorp Scientific, a biotech venture studio, proudly announces the official emergence of GENOS, marking its decisive step out of stealth-mode and into the limelight of pioneering bioinformatics solutions. Spearheaded by Dr. Osvaldo D. Rivera, a Bioinformatics Scientist and Cancer Cell Biologist, GENOS aims to revolutionize the landscape of bioinformatics services by providing accessible solutions to investigator's most complex projects and biological data hurdles.
