Ozcorp Scientific Completes Registration in Puerto Rico to Invest in the Region's Biotech Economy and Catalyze Innovative Ventures

December 08, 2023 – Press
GUAYNABO, PUERTO RICO – Ozcorp Scientific, a biotech venture studio, has announced the successful completion of its registration process in Puerto Rico, marking a significant milestone in the company's strategic roadmap and commitment to fostering the island's burgeoning biotech sector.

This milestone achievement underscores Ozcorp Scientific's dedication to advancing scientific research and development while actively contributing to Puerto Rico's growing biotechnology landscape. By establishing a presence in Puerto Rico, the company aims to leverage the region's robust infrastructure, skilled talent pool, and favorable regulatory environment to drive innovation and create economic opportunities within the biotech industry.

We are thrilled to finalize our corporate registration in Puerto Rico, a vibrant hub for biotechnology.


– Osvaldo D. Rivera, PhD.
Founder & Managing Director
Ozcorp Scientific LLC


Ozcorp Scientific was originally registered in the state of Pennsylvania back in 2019, later temporarily sustaining operations in Texas, and has been operating mostly in stealth until recently while it was establishing internal procedures and acquiring specialized business expertise. Ozcorp is now expanding its horizons and visibility, leveraging the developed foundation to step into new territories and engage with the broader biotech community. The focus remains on establishing meaningful connections and collaborations that fuel innovative biotechnologies.

The move comes amidst Puerto Rico's recent designation by the White House as 1 of 31 Regional Tech Hubs to Spur American Innovation, "This strategic move aligns with our vision to not only advance groundbreaking research but also to collaborate with local innovators, founders, universities, and government entities to fuel the growth of the biotech ecosystem in Puerto Rico." – said Dr. Rivera, Founder of Ozcorp Scientific. "We've leveraged the resources of SpecePR to provide a lean option for designated office space whilst most of our operations remain remote and digital."

Ozcorp Scientific's entry into Puerto Rico reflects a commitment to catalyzing economic development by fostering partnerships and investing in cutting-edge technologies. The company anticipates that its presence will facilitate job creation and pave the way for collaborations that drive scientific breakthroughs, benefiting both the local community and the global scientific community.

As Ozcorp Scientific embarks on this new chapter, it remains steadfast in its mission to push the boundaries of biotechnology, delivering innovative solutions that address critical healthcare, agricultural, and environmental challenges.
